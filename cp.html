Austria Overview

The Austrian Capital is Vienna, and the official language is German. Austria has been an EU member country since 1 January 1995. There are 8,9 million inhabitants in Austria, distributed over 83,8 thousand km2. The Austrian GDP per inhabitant equals 44,8 thousand Euro in 2019.
